Deluxe Tasbih

Developed in Jan. 2021

Deluxe Tasbih is an easy-to-use iPhone app designed to help users keep track of their daily prayers and spiritual routines. The app features a digital tasbih counter, customizable main screen tools, and a variety of helpful widgets that make tracking effortless.

  • Digital tasbih counter with tactile UI feedback
  • Multiple widget sizes for quick access from the home screen
  • Customizable main screen tools and appearance
  • Lightweight, battery-friendly implementation

The app was built as a focused utility for users who want a simple, reliable digital tasbih. It emphasizes clarity, quick interactions, and useful widgets so users can keep their flow without distractions.
