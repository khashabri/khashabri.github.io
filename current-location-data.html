Privacy Policy

This app is designed to provide accurate, real-time location data without collecting unnecessary personal information.

What data is used:
• The app accesses your device’s location only to display your current position, speed, direction, and weather.
• GPS coordinates, city, and country names are shown locally on your device.
• Weather data is retrieved for your current location, but no personal identifiers are sent or stored.

What is not collected:
• No accounts, sign-ins, or personal profiles are required.
• The app does not collect, transmit, or store your location history.
• No personal data, contacts, photos, or other device information is accessed.
• No advertising or analytics trackers are used.

Your privacy:
All location and speed data is processed locally on your device and is never sent to any server. You are in full control of your information. For questions or support, please contact khashabri@hotmail.com.
