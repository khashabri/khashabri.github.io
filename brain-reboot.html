Privacy Policy

Brain Reboot is an iOS app designed to help you relax and reset your mind. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data collection:
• Brain Reboot does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
• No accounts or sign-ins are required.
• No location, contacts, photos, or any other device information is accessed.
• No advertising or third-party analytics trackers are used.

Apple developer statistics:
• Apple may collect limited, anonymized usage statistics (such as crash reports and download counts) through the App Store as part of their standard developer program. This data is aggregated, cannot be used to identify you, and is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Summary:
Brain Reboot itself collects absolutely no data. Everything happens on your device. For questions or support, please contact khashabri@hotmail.com.
