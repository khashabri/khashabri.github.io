BetterCast

Podcast player for iPhone, iPad & Apple Watch

About the App

BetterCast is a fast, intuitive, and completely ad-free podcast player built for people who truly love podcasts. It is designed from the ground up to make every part of podcast listening smarter, smoother, and more enjoyable across Apple devices.

  • 100% ad-free experience — no banners, no in-app interruptions
  • Advanced playback: speed control, skip silence, chapter markers, episode notes
  • On-device transcription for any podcast, with privacy in mind
  • Powerful playlist customization by keyword, topic, and duration
  • Detailed listening insights and storage analytics
  • Deep Apple ecosystem integration — iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and iMessage

Privacy Policy / Datenschutz

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how BetterCast handles your data.

Data collection:
• BetterCast does not collect, sell, or transmit personal data to the developer.
• No account or sign-in is required to use the app.
• No advertising SDKs and no third-party analytics or tracking are used.
• Podcast transcription runs on your device; audio is not uploaded to us for transcription.

Data stored on your device and in your iCloud:
• Your subscriptions, playlists, playback progress, and transcripts stay on your device.
• If iCloud sync is enabled, this data is stored in your own private iCloud account and is not accessible to the developer. It is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Third-party podcast content:
• To play and download episodes, the app fetches RSS feeds and audio directly from the podcast publishers’ servers. Those publishers may receive standard network request information (such as your IP address) under their own privacy policies, as is normal for any podcast app.

Apple developer statistics:
• Apple may collect limited, anonymized, aggregated usage statistics (such as crash reports and download counts) through the App Store as part of their standard developer program. This data cannot be used to identify you.

Summary:
BetterCast itself collects no personal data — everything happens on your device or in your own iCloud. For any privacy questions, contact khashabri@hotmail.com.
